# NovaCRM Technologies

# Customer Success Playbook

Version: 1.0

Confidential – Internal Use Only

---

# 1. Purpose

The Customer Success Playbook provides standardized guidelines for Customer Success Managers (CSMs) to improve customer adoption, increase customer satisfaction, reduce churn, and maximize long-term customer value.

The objective is to ensure every customer receives consistent service throughout their lifecycle.

---

# 2. Customer Lifecycle

Every customer passes through the following stages:

1. Onboarding
2. Product Adoption
3. Value Realization
4. Expansion
5. Renewal
6. Advocacy

Customer Success Managers are responsible for monitoring customer progress during each stage.

---

# 3. Customer Health Score

Customer Health Score is measured on a scale of 0–100.

| Health Score | Status | Action |
|--------------|--------|----------------------------|
| 80–100 | Healthy | Continue engagement |
| 60–79 | Stable | Monitor regularly |
| 40–59 | At Risk | Schedule Customer Review |
| Below 40 | Critical | Executive Escalation |

Health score is calculated using:

- Product Usage
- Login Frequency
- Support Tickets
- Customer Satisfaction
- Renewal Readiness

---

# 4. Early Churn Indicators

The following are considered early warning signs:

- Product usage decreases by more than 30%
- Customer misses Quarterly Business Review
- Multiple unresolved support tickets
- Negative meeting sentiment
- Competitor mentioned during meetings
- Renewal discussions delayed
- Decision maker becomes inactive

Whenever two or more indicators appear together, the account should be classified as High Risk.

---

# 5. Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

QBR meetings should be conducted every quarter.

Objectives include:

- Review customer goals
- Present ROI
- Discuss challenges
- Introduce new features
- Plan future roadmap

---

# 6. Expansion Opportunities

Expansion opportunities should be explored when:

- Customer Health Score > 80
- Product adoption exceeds 75%
- Customer requests additional users
- Customer requests additional integrations

Possible expansion options:

- Upgrade subscription
- AI Analytics Module
- Automation Package
- Enterprise Security Package

---

# 7. Customer Communication Best Practices

Customer Success Managers should:

- Respond within one business day
- Maintain professional communication
- Provide proactive updates
- Document every customer interaction
- Follow up after major meetings

Avoid:

- Delayed responses
- Unclear commitments
- Untracked action items

---

# 8. Executive Escalation

Executive escalation is required when:

- Health Score < 40
- Renewal due within 30 days
- Customer expresses intent to leave
- Strategic enterprise customer reports critical issues

Executive participants may include:

- VP Customer Success
- Director of Customer Success
- Product Manager
- Engineering Lead

---

# 9. Success Metrics

Customer Success Managers are evaluated using:

- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Customer Health Score
- Product Adoption Rate
- Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
- Renewal Rate
- Expansion Revenue

---

# 10. Summary

Customer Success is focused on long-term customer outcomes rather than short-term support.

Every interaction should improve customer health, strengthen relationships, and increase business value.
