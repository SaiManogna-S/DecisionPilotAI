# NovaCRM Technologies

# Renewal Policy

Version: 1.0

Confidential – Internal Use Only

---

# 1. Purpose

The Renewal Policy defines the standard renewal process for all NovaCRM customers.

The objective is to maximize renewal rates while ensuring customer satisfaction.

---

# 2. Renewal Timeline

| Days Before Renewal | Activity |
|--------------------|----------|
| 120 | Account Review |
| 90 | Customer Health Assessment |
| 60 | Renewal Discussion |
| 30 | Final Proposal |
| 15 | Executive Escalation (if required) |

---

# 3. Customer Health Rules

| Health Score | Action |
|--------------|---------------------------|
| 80-100 | Auto Renewal Recommendation |
| 60-79 | Standard Renewal Process |
| 40-59 | Customer Success Intervention |
| Below 40 | Executive Escalation Required |

---

# 4. High-Risk Accounts

Accounts are classified as High Risk when:

- Health Score below 60
- Product adoption below 50%
- Three or more unresolved support tickets
- Negative meeting sentiment
- Competitor mentioned during renewal discussions

---

# 5. Executive Escalation

Executive approval is required when:

- Enterprise customer
- Health Score below 40
- Requested discount above 20%
- Renewal value exceeds ₹25,00,000 annually

---

# 6. Renewal Best Practices

Customer Success Managers should:

- Schedule renewal discussions early
- Review customer goals
- Demonstrate business value
- Address open issues before renewal
- Provide success metrics

---

# 7. Renewal Outcomes

Possible outcomes:

- Renew Existing Plan
- Upgrade Subscription
- Downgrade Subscription
- Churn
- Expansion Opportunity

---

# 8. Summary

Renewals should always be proactive, data-driven, and customer-focused.
