# NovaCRM Technologies

# Support Standard Operating Procedure

Version: 1.0

Confidential – Internal Use Only

---

# 1. Incident Priorities

| Priority | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| P1 | 30 Minutes | 4 Hours |
| P2 | 2 Hours | 24 Hours |
| P3 | 8 Hours | 3 Days |
| P4 | 1 Business Day | 5 Days |

---

# 2. Escalation Rules

Escalate immediately when:

- Enterprise customer reports a P1 issue
- Customer experiences complete service outage
- Security incident reported
- Multiple customers affected

---

# 3. Communication Guidelines

Support Engineers must:

- Acknowledge every ticket.
- Provide updates every 4 hours for P1 incidents.
- Document every action taken.
- Confirm resolution before closing the ticket.

---

# 4. Engineering Escalation

Engineering involvement is required for:

- Product bugs
- API failures
- Performance degradation
- Database issues

---

# 5. Customer Satisfaction

After ticket closure:

- Send customer survey.
- Collect CSAT score.
- Identify improvement opportunities.

---

# 6. Summary

Customer communication is as important as technical resolution.
